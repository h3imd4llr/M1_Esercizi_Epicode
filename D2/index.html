Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oline oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flous is fully absorbed.
